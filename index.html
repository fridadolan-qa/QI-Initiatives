QI Initiative Tracker

How to submit, track, and close the loop on Quality Intelligence findings

QI Team
Samsara Support Quality
What this is: A shared dashboard where QA team members log findings from audits, DSAT reviews, and AutoQA data — and track whether the business acted on them. Every initiative ties a QI insight to a KPI, a recommended action, and a quantified business opportunity. The goal is to stop insights from going dark after delivery.
The four categories
PeopleAgent behavior, coaching gaps, performance patterns
ProcessWorkflow breakdowns, routing issues, SOP gaps
ProductTooling issues, macro problems, system defects
PolicyRule gaps, inconsistent guidance, compliance risk
Effort tiers
Project2+ weeks or 10+ hours — full scoped work
Complex task1–2 weeks or 5–10 hours — multi-step
Continuous improvement≤1 week or ≤5 hours — contained fix
Quick win<1 day or <2 hours — fast action
The quantified opportunity field is non-negotiable

This is what makes a finding actionable for Directors+. Don't submit without it. Include at least one of: ticket volume affected, rate delta, projected score change, or estimated cost impact. Example: "~85 tickets/week misrouted · est. 12pt escalation rate reduction · $18k AHT savings"

Team norms
→Submit within the same week you identify the finding — don't batch
→If you're blocked on quantifying, ask Frida before leaving the field blank
→Update the status yourself when it changes — don't wait for someone to ask
→If help is needed from another team, flag it in the tracker — don't just mention it in Slack
→The dashboard is visible to Directors+ — write the insight and recommended action as if they're reading it directly
→When a Jira ticket exists, link it in the dashboard row — keeps the paper trail intact
